About us

Welcome to Comtech shop inc., your number one source for all your computer needs. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of different computer parts and accessories, with a focus on quality, accessibility, and guranteed customer satisfaction.

Founded in 2020 by Ichigo Kurosaki, Comtech shop inc. has come a long way from its beginnings in Manila, Philippines. When Ichigo first started out, his passion for building computers drove them to create modern computer accessories so that Comtech shop inc. can offer you the highest quality of computer tech that you need. We now serve customers all over the world, and are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into our own website.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us. Sincerely, Ichigo Kurosaki
